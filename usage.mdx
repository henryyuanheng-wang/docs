## View usage

To track usage of Teamate, go to `https://my.teamate.app/usage` .

You will see analytics and metrics about Teamate activity in your workspace.

![Usage dashboard](/images/usage_dashboard.png)

## Manage Channels

Click "Manage" button to view joined channels, customize Teamate's behavior per channel, or leave a channel.

![Usage dashboard](/images/manage_channels.png)
